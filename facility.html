Safety, Health & Environment Rule.

Have priority to Health, Safety and Environment. Our workers are strictly instructed to comply with all the safety regulations.

WE EMPHASIE THE FOLLOWING:

- Encourage safe activities in overall activities

- Promote employees who earn safety recognition from clients

- Aware successful turnover of works depend on safe activities which is the significant factor of client requirement.

- All of our workers are instructed to report any unsafe act to the supervisor.

- Make sure safety prior to commencing any job. - Delay to work owing to safety concerns shall not be accounted.
